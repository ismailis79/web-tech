Player Eligibility

Please review the eligibility guidelines below to ensure you register for the correct division.

Division 1 (Pure)

  • Single-school teams
  • Players must attend and represent the school they register under
  • Applies to schools with sufficient roster size

Division 2 (Combined)

  • For schools without enough players for a full roster
  • Players registered in the Combined division will be placed on a team based on their school, or by home address for homeschool and academy students
  • Teams may combine players from multiple nearby schools
